Our Story

Strengthening India's Power Distribution Network Since 2016

ACE Industries was established in 2016 with a clear objective — to strengthen the power distribution network with reliable switchgear and tamper-proof enclosures for low and medium-voltage applications.

Our deep-drawn and fabricated sheet-metal facility quickly became a favourite among electrical utilities for housing electronic energy meters of every size and rating. The deep-drawn metering cabinets prevent pilferage through the sides and corners of the cubicle and deliver unmatched dust and water protection.

The metallic enclosure not only safeguards against unauthorised access but also eliminates the most common methods of power theft — including the application of strong magnets and the injection of high-frequency electromagnetic waves — helping our customers minimise T&D losses.

What Drives Us

Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

To be an innovative, entrepreneurial and empowered team that constantly creates value and attains global benchmarks — increasing industrial productivity, lowering environmental impact and emerging as a world leader in dedicated power-distribution equipment.

Our Mission

To work closely with our customers to understand their needs, and to provide shockproof, theft-proof distribution panels and meter boxes — striving to become the largest enclosure company in India and to make India a power-theft-free, self-dependent nation.

Our Values

Our values lie in quality-management policies that ensure customer satisfaction, reduce cycle time and cost, and eliminate errors and re-work. We are committed to enhancing our manufacturing and R&D capability, and to treating customers, employees and suppliers exactly as we wish to be treated.
