Manufacturing Technologies

Four Materials, Engineered for Every Environment

Select the optimal enclosure technology for your application — we manufacture all four in-house.

Deep Drawn

Seamless single-piece steel forming for maximum strength and water-tightness — ideal for outdoor LT enclosures.

Fabricated

CNC-formed and welded sheet-steel enclosures for custom sizes, ratings and complex feeder pillar configurations.

SMC

Sheet Moulding Compound — fully corrosion-proof, UV-stable, flame-retardant and maintenance-free composite.

Polycarbonate

Transparent, impact-resistant and RF-friendly — the smart choice for smart meter and prepaid metering boxes.
