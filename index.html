About ACE Industries

Engineering Trust into Every Enclosure We Build

ACE Industries is a leading ISO 9001 & NSIC certified manufacturer of electrical distribution and metering enclosure solutions for India's power sector.

Established in 2016, we engineer deep-drawn and fabricated LT distribution boxes, CT & protection meter boxes, feeder pillars and customized enclosures for state electricity boards, DISCOMs, EPC contractors and infrastructure developers. Our tamper-proof, theft-proof metering cabinets are engineered to eliminate power pilferage and minimise T&D losses.

  • Tamper-proof & theft-proof

    Metallic cabinets resist magnet & high-frequency tampering; IP-55 rated.

  • Four manufacturing technologies

    Deep Drawn, Fabricated, SMC and Polycarbonate under one roof.

  • Statewide supply network

    Reliable, on-time delivery to project sites across India.
