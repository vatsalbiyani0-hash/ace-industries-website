Testing Facilities

An In-House Laboratory You Can Rely On

Our quality testing laboratory validates every enclosure against applicable IS standards and utility specifications before it leaves the plant.

  • IP Protection Testing

    Dust and water ingress validation to IP54 / IP55 / IP65.

  • Dielectric & Insulation

    High-voltage and insulation resistance verification.

  • Mechanical & Corrosion

    Impact, load and salt-spray testing for durability.

  • Dimensional Inspection

    Precision measurement against approved drawings.
